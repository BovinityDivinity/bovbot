Thanksgiving Holiday

The following libraries in the Lake County Library System will be closed from Thursday, Nov. 23 until Monday, Nov. 27 for Thanksgiving:

• Astor County Library (early closure at 5 p.m. on Wednesday)
• Cagan Crossings Community Library in Clermont (early closure at 5 p.m. on Wednesday)
• Cooper Memorial Library in Clermont (also closed Wednesday)
• East Lake County Library in Sorrento (early closure at 5 p.m. on Wednesday)
• Fruitland Park Library (early closure on Monday at 6 p.m., Tuesday at 5 p.m. and Wednesday at 3 p.m.)
• Helen Lehmann Memorial Library in Montverde
• Lady Lake Public Library
• Marianne Beck Memorial Library in Howey-in-the-Hills
• Marion Baysinger Memorial Library in Groveland (early closure at 5 p.m. on Wednesday)
• Minneola Schoolhouse Library
• Paisley County Library (early closure at 5 p.m. on Wednesday)
• Tavares Public Library

The Leesburg Public Library will close at 6 p.m. on Wednesday, Nov. 22 and reopen on Saturday, Nov. 25. The Umatilla Public Library, and W. T. Bland Public Library be closed on Thursday and Friday but will be open during normal operational hours on Saturday, with the W. T. Bland Public Library also open on Sunday.
